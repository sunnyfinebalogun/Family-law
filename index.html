About This Project

The Family Law Room blends modern access with traditional chambers-inspired insight. It offers practical tools, court-prep materials, and reflections rooted in UK family law — crafted for those representing themselves and the professionals who support them.

It is led by a Bar Training Course student who believes the legal process should serve people — not overwhelm them.
